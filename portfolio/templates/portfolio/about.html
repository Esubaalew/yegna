The Yegna Farms Model

Our comprehensive approach addresses multiple aspects of rural women's empowerment through a proven, scalable model.

100 Productive Chickens

Carefully selected, locally adapted chickens that thrive in Ethiopian conditions and maximize egg production.

Capacity Building

Comprehensive on-the-job training in poultry husbandry, home economics, and business management.

Saving & Credit

Financial empowerment program enabling households to save 2,000 Birr per month through structured savings.

Household Wellness

Comprehensive solutions for household sanitation and nutrition to improve overall family well-being.

Value Addition

Complete value chain support: egg collection, aggregation, grading, packaging, labeling, and market linkage.

Partnership Network

Strategic partnerships with government, international organizations, and financial institutions.
